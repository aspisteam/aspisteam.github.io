What do we do?

We provide real time fleet management tools
We turn assets to localized weather data collection points
We analyze the collected and historic data to provide fire behavior predictions and help C&C adapt their strategy in real time
We store the locally gathered data to be analyzed for future improvements in firefighting techniques
